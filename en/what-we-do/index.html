SMART SEARCH

Smart search for websites, knowledge bases and internal repositories no longer has to depend only on fulltext and manually created synonym lists. Thanks to the development of neural networks semantic search is growing in importance. Semantic search works with user intent, meaning of words and context. It encodes meaning of text into numerical vectors and is able to return relevant results even if there are no word matches. When multimedia search, personalization and AI features are added, the solution is able to keep pace with Google.

PROJECT SCHEDULING

Do you have a team that collaborates on different projects? Do you need to optimize your schedule to meet your deadlines? Scheduling is a very challenging task even for a small team with several projects. We have developed a system that will generate the optimal assignment of tasks to team members based on many different criteria such as qualifications, time availability, project priority, even distribution of work, and personal preferences.
