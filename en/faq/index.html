FAQ

How much does it cost?

We develop customized AI solutions, so each project is unique not only in its design but also in its price. In some companies, minor optimization of existing processes is enough, in others you need to come up with a completely new solution.

The price depends, for example, on whether you have all the data, whether you need to integrate with other systems or use different types of equipment. We can offer a reliable price estimate when we know the details of the project.

Do you have an idea and don't know how to implement it? Or do you have a problem and don't know how to solve it? Tell us more about your business and what you would like to improve. We will propose a solution and a price calculation taking into account your possibilities. Get in touch with us by phone, email or by filling in the contact form.

Who are our customers?

Anyone who wants to innovate. Companies from manufacturing, e-commerce, consulting, law firms and many other sectors. We are also open to opportunities for cooperation in academic research projects.

We are also open to collaboration with other software companies that do not specialize in AI, which we can help with implementation of smart AI modules.

In short, if you want to use AI, get in touch!

Who is AI suitable for?

We believe that every company will benefit from properly implemented AI. Artificial intelligence can be effectively integrated into many company processes from administration to production, logistics to communication with customers and internal communication.

We always look for the right AI solution to suit the individual needs of the client. Take a look at our examples of AI solutions.

What projects do we do most often?

The market is full of companies that have a ready-made product and your company is trying to fit it. We do it the other way around, we start with your business, your processes and plans and then we find out what will help you.

Because of our client focus, we don't have our own products, but if we find an existing product that would help you, we will point it out to you and help you incorporate it into your business. We don't create digital islands, but we strive to create ecosystems where information flows freely and is used to its full potential.

Do you prefer an agile approach or standard fixed price projects?

We set up our cooperation according to your needs and preferences. We can collaborate in the form of a classic fixed price project, where we provide you with an estimate of the workload of each part at the beginning and propose a project plan with milestones.

We are also happy to work in an agile way. If you want to use a specific agile development methodology within the development process, we can use it.

How much does an initial consultation cost?

Initial consultations are non-binding and free of charge. We are happy to learn about your company or product and discuss the possibilities of implementing AI. To arrange a consultation, give us a call or send us a contact via the contact form.

How long does it take to implement AI into company processes?

It can take us a month or even a year to implement an AI solution. It always depends on the size of the project. However, the cooperation doesn't end with the handover of the project for us. Technology needs to be innovated and maintained, which is why we want to be available to you for the long-term. Take a look at how our collaboration would work.

What if it stops working over time?

We prefer long-term cooperation. Technologies need to be innovated and updated, which is why we will be available to you even after the handover of the finished project.

Do I need a dedicated employee to maintain the AI?

We create new solutions to fit into your company's existing processes. That's why it's important for us to work with your employees and experts, whom we will also teach how to work with the new technology. Even after the project has been handed over, you can always contact us and we will help.

Or we can even help you start your own AI team.

Isn't it better to buy a finished product?

Our services are unique in that we find the solution specifically for you. One that can be seamlessly integrated within all existing processes in the company. With off-the-shelf products, this can be a hassle. However, if there is already a suitable product on the market, we can help you implement it. We don't want to offer custom development if it's not needed. We are always looking for the most efficient and sustainable way for you to use AI.
